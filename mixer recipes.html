Mixer Recipes:

On this page I will be supplying you with a few recipes for mixers that will be good to know for a new bartender!        

  • Simple Syrup-
    1 ounce of tequila, .5 ounces of Triple Sec, and 6 ounces of Sour Mix. Add all liquids to your shaker and shake thoroughly with ice. After shaken, strain the contents into a glass with ice. You can add salt or tajin to the rim if requested.  
  •  
    Old Fashioned- 
    1.5 ounces of bourbon, 4 shakes of bitters, and a couple of drops of simple syrup. Muddle a orange in your shaker and then combine the rest of the contents with ice. Use a stirring spoon to thoroughly stir the contents. Once stirred, strain the contents into a rocks glass with ice and an orange peel.
  •  
    Dirty Vodka Martini-
    3 ounces of vodka, .5 ounces of Dry Vermouth, and 1 ounce of Olive Juice. Add your vodka and olive juice into your cocktail shaker and shake with ice. Swirl the vermouth around your cocktail glass until the inside is coated. Strain the contents of your shaker into the martini glass and garnish with one to two olives.
  •  Manhattan-
    3 ounces of bourbon, .5 ounces of Sweet Vermouth. Add your bourbon into your cocktail shaker and shake with ice. Swirl the sweet vermouth around your cocktail glass until the inside is coated. Strain the contents of your shaker into the martini glass and garnish with a cherry.
  •  
    Whiskey Sour-
    1.5 ounces of whiskey, 6 ounces of Sour Mix. Add your whiskey and sour mix into your cocktail shaker and shake with ice. Once thoroughly shaken, strain the contents of your shaker into a glass with ice.
  •  
    Dirty Shirley Temple- 
    1.5 ounces of vodka, 1 ounce of grenadine, and 8 ounces of lime-flavored soda. Add your vodka and soda into a glass with ice and add the grenadine last and garnish with a cherry.
  •  Vodka and Soda- 
    1.5 ounces of vodka and 8 ounces of soda water. Combine these ingredients into a glass with ice.
  •  
    Gin and Tonic-
    1.5 ounces of gin and 8 ounces of tonic water. Combine these ingredients into a glass with ice.
  •  
    Moscow Mule-
    1.5 ounces of vodka, .5 ounces of lime juic, 8 ounces of ginger beer. Add the ingredients into a copper mug with ice and garnish with a lime.
  •  Jack and Coke
    1.5 ounces of Jack Daniels and 8 ounces of Coca-Cola. Combine these ingredients into a glass with ice.
  •  
    Whiskey Ginger- 1.5 ounces of whiskey and 8 ounces of ginger ale. Combine these ingredients into a glass with ice.
