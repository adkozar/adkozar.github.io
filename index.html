Welcome!

 Hi Guys! My name is Andrew Kozar. I have been in the service industry for the last 15 years on-and-off. After working ten years as a chef I eventually decided to try something new. A friend of mine got me a job as an event bartender at a local catering service. It was a bit of a change at first but I ended up realizing a lot of the skills that you develop in the kitchen you can also use as a bartender. My goal for this website is to help others that may be new to the job. Even if you are not a new bartender hopefully I will still have some useful information for you!
